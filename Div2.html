Web 1-- Fundamentals of Website Development

"Web 1.0" refers to the early days of the World Wide Web, roughly from the inception of the internet in the early 1990s to around the late 2000s. During this period, websites were primarily static, meaning they were composed mainly of text and images that were displayed to users without much interactivity.

Web design encompasses a wide range of disciplines and skills used in the creation and maintenance of websites. It involves aspects such as graphic design, user interface (UI) design, user experience (UX) design, coding, and content creation. Effective web design aims to create visually appealing, user-friendly, and functional websites that meet the needs of both the site owner and its visitors.

NSTP 2-- Literacy Training Service 2

"Literacy Training Service 2" (LTS 2) is a program or course typically offered in educational institutions, particularly in the Philippines, as part of the National Service Training Program (NSTP).

The primary objective of LTS 2 is to enhance the literacy skills of students and empower them to become effective literacy trainers and advocates within their communities.
